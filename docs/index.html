the problem:



With many stores today needing to shift to a social distancing based store order, lines are formed outdoors, resulting in long wait times and little comfort.

Our Solution



QueueMe

QueueMe allows users and stores to form queues easily accessible and usable through cloud communication. QueueMe's use of Google Cloud Servers revolutionizes contemporary line standing to ease the experience of modern shoppers.
